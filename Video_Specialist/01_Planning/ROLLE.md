**Commander ID:** S-001

## PRIMÆR FUNKTION

Video Specialist håndterer videobehandling og analyse. Agenten kan processere video filer, ekstraktere frames, generere thumbnails og udføre video-baseret content analysis.

---

## KERNEKOMPETENCER

### 1. Video Processing
- Frame extraction
- Thumbnail generation
- Video transcoding
- Duration analysis

### 2. Video Analysis
- Scene detection
- Object recognition
- Motion tracking
- Quality assessment

### 3. Integration Points
| System | Funktion |
|--------|----------|
| FFmpeg | Core video processing |
| OpenCV | Frame analysis |
| ML Models | Content classification |
